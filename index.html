Junior Web Developer

A junior web developer is an entry-level professional who assists in the development and maintenance of websites and web applications. They typically work under the guidance of senior developers or team leads and contribute to various aspects of the development process. Junior web developers are responsible for writing code, debugging, testing, and implementing new features or functionalities. They often collaborate with designers and content creators to ensure a seamless user experience. Junior web developers are continuously learning and refining their skills to grow into more senior roles within the field of web development.
